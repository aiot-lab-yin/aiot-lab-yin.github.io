# About AIoT Laboratory

Welcome to the Artificial Intelligent IoT (AIoT) Laboratory at Reitaku University. Our lab focuses on the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Internet of Things technologies.

## Our Mission
To advance the field of intelligent connected systems through innovative research and education.

## Research Focus
- Edge AI and Intelligent Systems
- IoT Sensor Networks and Applications  
- Machine Learning for Embedded Devices
- Smart Environments and Automation
